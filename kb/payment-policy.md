# Payment Policy

## Overview

BeastLife offers multiple secure payment options for online purchases. All payments are processed in Indian Rupees (INR), and industry-standard encryption is used to protect payment information during transactions.

## Accepted Payment Methods

Customers can pay using:
- UPI (Unified Payments Interface)
- Credit Cards
- Debit Cards
- Net Banking
- Cash on Delivery (COD) with partial prepaid payment

## Cash on Delivery (COD)

For COD orders, BeastLife requires a nominal prepaid confirmation fee at the time of placing the order. This amount is **non-refundable**, even if:
- The order is refused at delivery
- The order is not accepted
- The customer chooses not to receive the product

## Payment Security

BeastLife uses secure payment processing and encryption to protect customer payment information. Credit and debit card details are not stored on the company's servers.

## Payment Processing

Payments are processed immediately after an order is placed. Customers receive an order confirmation email containing their payment and order details.

## Currency

All transactions are processed in **Indian Rupees (INR)**. International customers may be charged according to their bank's currency conversion rates.

## Payment Issues

Customers experiencing payment failures or checkout issues should contact BeastLife customer support for assistance.

## Refunds and Cancellations

Unless covered under the Returns Policy, completed payments and confirmed orders are generally considered final. Customers should carefully review their order before completing payment.
