## Frequently Asked Questions

### Who should use a mass gainer?
Mass gainers are suitable for people who struggle to consume enough calories through food or are trying to gain weight and build muscle.

### Does this mass gainer contain creatine?
Yes. Each serving includes added creatine to support strength and muscle performance.

### Does it contain maltodextrin?
No. BeastLife Anabolic Mass Gainer is formulated without maltodextrin and uses a millet-based carbohydrate blend.

### When should I take a mass gainer?
It can be consumed after workouts or between meals to increase total daily calorie intake.

### Can beginners use a mass gainer?
Yes. Beginners who find it difficult to gain weight or meet their calorie needs can use a mass gainer alongside a balanced diet and regular training.
