# Privacy Policy

## Overview

BeastLife collects personal information that users voluntarily provide, such as their name, email address, phone number, and other details required to process orders, provide customer support, and improve user experience. By using the website, users consent to the collection and processing of this information in accordance with the company's privacy policy.

## Information Collected

- Personal information provided during registration or checkout
- Order and transaction details
- Device information such as IP address, browser type, operating system, and internet service provider
- Website usage data collected through cookies and similar technologies

## How Information Is Used

Collected information may be used to:
- Process and deliver orders
- Provide customer support
- Improve website functionality and user experience
- Analyze website performance and usage trends
- Send service-related communications and promotional offers (where applicable)

## Cookies

The website uses cookies to improve functionality, remember user preferences, analyze traffic, and enhance the browsing experience. Third-party service providers may also use cookies for analytics and advertising.

## Information Sharing

BeastLife does not sell personally identifiable information. Information may be shared with trusted service providers, business partners, or legal authorities when necessary to operate the business, fulfill services, comply with legal obligations, or protect user rights.

## User Rights

Users may request to access, update, or correct their personal information. Consent for data processing may be withdrawn, although this may affect the availability of certain services.

## Third-Party Services

The website may contain links to third-party websites or services. Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies, and BeastLife is not responsible for their handling of user data.

## Data Security

Reasonable measures are implemented to protect personal information, although no internet transmission or electronic storage method can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

## Applicable Law

BeastLife states that its privacy practices comply with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (India).
