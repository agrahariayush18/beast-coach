# Returns Policy

## Overview

BeastLife accepts returns only under specific conditions. Most products are not eligible for returns or exchanges unless they meet the company's return criteria.

## Return Eligibility

A return request must be submitted within **2 days of delivery** by emailing **care@beastlife.in**.

Products are eligible for return only if:
- The product itself is damaged or tampered with.
- Damage is not limited to the outer shipping box.
- The company approves the return after review.

## Required Documents

To request a return, customers must provide:
- A complete unpackaging video showing the product from opening to inspection.
- The original Order ID.
- Supporting evidence of product damage.

Without an unpackaging video, return requests will not be considered.

## Apparel & Accessories

Apparel and accessories must be:
- Unworn
- Unwashed
- In original packaging
- With all original tags attached

If received damaged, an unpackaging video is also required.

## Refund Process

Once a return is approved and received:
- Refunds are issued to the original payment method.
- Processing typically takes **14–21 business days** after the returned package is received.

## Important Notes

- Return approval is at BeastLife's discretion.
- Return requests submitted after the 2-day window are not accepted.
- Email all return requests to **care@beastlife.in**.
