## Frequently Asked Questions

### What are BCAAs?
Branched-Chain Amino Acids (BCAAs) are three essential amino acids—Leucine, Isoleucine, and Valine—that support muscle protein synthesis and recovery. Since the body cannot produce them, they must be obtained through food or supplements.

### What are the benefits of BCAAs?
BCAA supplementation may help:
- Support muscle growth
- Reduce muscle soreness
- Improve recovery after exercise
- Preserve lean muscle during calorie restriction
- Improve workout endurance

### Who should take BCAAs?
BCAAs are suitable for:
- Strength athletes
- Bodybuilders
- Endurance athletes
- CrossFit athletes
- Individuals looking to improve recovery and reduce muscle soreness

### When should I take BCAAs?
BCAAs can be consumed before, during, or after workouts. They may also be taken between meals to help maintain amino acid intake.

### Can I take BCAAs on rest days?
Yes. They can be consumed on rest days to support recovery and help maintain your overall amino acid intake.

### Are BCAAs suitable for beginners?
Yes. Beginners and experienced athletes alike can use BCAAs to support muscle recovery alongside a balanced diet and regular training.
