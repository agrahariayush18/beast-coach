# Shipping Policy

## Order Processing

Orders are generally processed within **0–7 business days**. Orders are not processed or shipped on weekends or public holidays.

During periods of high order volume, shipment processing may take additional time.

## Delivery

Most orders are delivered within the estimated delivery timeline, although delays may occur depending on location, courier service, or order volume.

## Shipment Tracking

After an order is shipped, customers receive a shipment confirmation email containing a tracking number. Tracking information usually becomes active within **24 hours**.

## Shipping Charges

Shipping charges, if applicable, are calculated during checkout. Orders meeting the company's free-shipping criteria may qualify for free delivery.

## Damaged Shipments

If a shipment arrives damaged during transit:
- Contact the shipping carrier to file a claim.
- Keep all packaging materials and damaged products until the claim is completed.

## Support

For shipping-related questions or order assistance, customers can contact BeastLife customer support through the official support channels.
